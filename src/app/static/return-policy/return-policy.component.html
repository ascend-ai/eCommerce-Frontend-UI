Refund/Return Policy

Thank you for shopping with us at Style it by Rose. We strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with our unique handmade jewellery pieces. Please read our refund and return policy below.

  1. Refunds

    • We offer refunds on items returned within 7 days of delivery.
    • To be eligible for a refund, items must be unused and in the same condition as received.
    • We accept returns if you have an uncut opening video of opening the parcel and after sending us the whole video we will look into it and if there are any damages or products missing from our end we will initiate partial refund of that or resend the new order to you.
    • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
    • Shipping charges are non-refundable, and customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error (e.g., wrong item shipped).

  2. Exchanges

    • We offer exchanges for items returned within 7 days of delivery.
    • Exchanges are subject to availability. If the item requested for exchange is not available, a refund will be processed as per our refund policy.
    • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs for exchanges unless the exchange is due to our error (e.g., wrong item shipped).

  3. How to Initiate a Refund or Exchange

    To initiate a refund or exchange, please contact us through the given contact number on our website or email us at styleitbyrose@gmail.com with your order number and details of the issue.

  4. Custom Orders

    Custom orders are non-refundable and non-exchangeable unless there is a defect or error on our part.

  5. Cancellation Policy

    Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of purchase. Please contact us as soon as possible if you wish to cancel your order.

  6. Refund Processing Time

    Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days of receiving the returned item.

This policy is effective as it applies to all purchases made through our website. We reserve the right to amend this policy at any time without prior notice.
